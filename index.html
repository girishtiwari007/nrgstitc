⚠️ Key Ambiguity (MB Division letter 15/09/2025 + RBA 19/2024): HSN/SAC 9954 covers both immovable property (T3 — blocked) and Plant & Machinery works contracts (T4 — eligible). Finance Act 2025 Sec 124 retrospectively changes "plant or machinery" to "plant and machinery" from 01.07.2017 — nullifying Safari Retreats SC ruling for building-as-plant argument. The single deciding test: Is the asset Plant & Machinery (P&M), or is it an immovable structure?

🚫 T3 — Blocked u/s 17(5)(c)/(d)

  • 995411 — Staff quarters / single dwelling
  • 995412 — Railway colonies / multi-dwelling
  • 995413 — Office buildings, schools, hospitals on railway land
  • 995419 — Other building construction
  • 995421 — ROB, FOB, LHS, flyovers (immovable civil structure)
  • 995422 — Water/sewer/pipeline infrastructure
  • 995429 — Boundary walls, culverts, misc civil works
  • 9954 — AMC of buildings (capitalised repair) [Finance Act 2025]
  • 9954 — Revenue repair of buildings (works contract on immovable)

Legal: Sec 17(5)(c) — WC for immovable property. Sec 17(5)(d) — goods/services for immovable property on own account. Finance Act 2025 Sec 124 clarifies retrospectively from 01.07.2017.

✅ T4 — P&M Exception to Sec 17(5)

  • 9954 (P&M WC) — Works contract for Plant & Machinery: signalling, workshop equipment, traction, OHE
  • 995466 — Lift installation at stations, FOBs, subways
  • 995467 — Escalator installation
  • 9954 — Kavach / IBS / ASC / signalling equipment installation
  • 9987 — AMC of P&M (workshop equipment, loco depot)
  • 9954 (OHE new) — New OHE/traction installation (treated as P&M)

Legal: Exception in Sec 17(5)(c) — "works contract services when supplied for construction of an immovable property (other than plant and machinery)". P&M exception confirmed by Finance Act 2025.

⚡ Ambiguous — Examine Each Bill

  • 995421 (ROB/FOB with embedded S&T) — civil portion T3; equipment P&M portion T4
  • 995423 — HT lines/OFC cables: T3 if permanently fixed to land; T4 if P&M per CBIC 219/2024
  • 9954 (P.Way service) — Service component C2; goods component T3
  • 9954 (OHE repair) — Service component C2; goods component T3
  • 9954 (S&T repair) — Service component C2; goods component T3

Split bills: always separate goods and service components. CBIC Circular 219/13/2024: ducts/manholes for OFC = plant & machinery — ITC eligible even if immovable.

📌 Capital vs Revenue & Deposit Works: ITC flag at IPAS must match the fund allocation. Capital works (Plan Heads) creating new assets → evaluate if P&M (T4) or immovable structure (T3). Revenue works (Demand 3–13, Working Expenses) → maintenance/repair: services → C2, materials → T3. Deposit works (third-party funded via deposit account) → same ITC rules apply based on nature of asset created.

🏗️ Capital Fund Works (Plan Heads 1100–6300)

  • New line / doubling / gauge conversion (civil) → T3
  • New station building / FOB / ROB (structure) → T3
  • New signalling / Kavach installation → T4 (P&M)
  • New OHE / traction electrification → T4 (P&M)
  • Workshop P&M / lathe / CNC installation → T4 (P&M)
  • Lifts & escalators at stations → T4 (P&M)
  • New rolling stock procurement (AC/freight) → T4
  • Staff quarters / colonies (capital) → T3
  • DRF replacement of immovable structure → T3
  • DRF replacement of P&M / rolling stock → T4

🔧 Revenue Working Expenses (Demands 3–13)

  • P.Way maintenance — service portion → C2
  • P.Way maintenance — materials/goods → T3
  • OHE / S&T repair — service → C2
  • OHE / S&T repair — goods/materials → T3
  • Loco / coach / wagon repair (workshop services) → C2
  • Workshop repair goods (stock items) → T3
  • AC coach maintenance (dedicated) → T4
  • Station cleaning / security / OBHS (common) → C2
  • Building maintenance / repair (any) → T3
  • Non-AC operations (direct exclusive inputs) → T2
  • Staff welfare / medical / school → T1

💰 Deposit Works (Third-Party Funded)

  • ROB / RUB deposit (State Govt) → T3
    Asset = immovable bridge. Railways executes as agency. T3 at IR IPAS stage.
  • Electrification deposit (State / SPV) → T4
    OHE/traction = P&M. T4 eligible if used for taxable output.
  • Signalling deposit (Kavach/IBS) → T4
    P&M equipment. Full ITC.
  • RVNL / DFCCIL / IRCON works → EX
    CBIC 228/2024: exempt from GST w.e.f. 15.07.2024. Regularised 01.07.2017. No GST on inward supply → no ITC question.
  • Ministry of Defence deposit — immovable → T3
  • Ministry of Defence deposit — P&M/equipment → T4

⚖️ Capitalised vs Revenue Repair

  • Capitalised repair (repair that increases asset life / creates new capacity, capitalised in books) → treated as construction → T3
    Finance Act 2025 Sec 124: "construction includes renovation to the extent capitalised"
  • Revenue repair (routine, not capitalised) → works contract on immovable → T3 (service); goods → T3
  • Revenue repair of P&M (service portion) → C2
  • Revenue repair of P&M (goods/materials) → T3 (RBA 19/2024)
  • New P&M under capital → T4 (exclusively taxable) or C2 (mixed AC+NonAC)

1

Is the inward supply for a NON-BUSINESS purpose?

Schools, hospitals, dispensaries, colony welfare, staff recreation, sports — no nexus with railway transport operations or any taxable output supply.

→ T1 (No ITC)→ NO: proceed to Step 2
2

Is it used EXCLUSIVELY for exempt passenger service (Non-AC / Sleeper / 2nd class)?

Inputs that go only into Non-AC class coaches or exclusively for exempt operations. Must be clearly identifiable as exclusively for exempt supply — if any doubt, go to Step 5.

→ T2 (No ITC)→ NO: proceed to Step 3
3

Is it listed in Section 17(5) blocked categories?

  • Motor vehicles / rent-a-cab / staff car hire [Sec 17(5)(a)/(b)]
  • Works contract for IMMOVABLE property [Sec 17(5)(c)] — except P&M
  • Goods / services for construction of immovable property on own account [Sec 17(5)(d)] — except P&M (Finance Act 2025 — "plant and machinery" not "plant or machinery")
  • Food & beverages / outdoor catering / club membership / beauty treatment [Sec 17(5)(b)]
  • Health / life insurance of employees [Sec 17(5)(b)(iii)] — unless mandated by law
  • Leave Travel Concession (LTC) [Sec 17(5)(b)]
  • Goods for repair of wagons/coaches/locos in workshop [RBA 19/2024 Sl.3]
  • Goods for construction of P&M (tracks, sleepers, signalling, cranes) [RBA 19/2024 Sl.7]
  • Building / colony AMC (to extent capitalised in books) [Finance Act 2025 Sec 124]
  • GTA services at 5% GST rate [RBA 19/2024 Sl.9]
→ T3 (No ITC — Blocked)→ NOT blocked: proceed to Step 4
3A

Is the inward supply EXEMPT under CBIC Circular 228/2024 or Notification 04/2024-CT(R)?

  • Services by SPVs (RVNL, DFCCIL) to IR during concession period
  • Inter-zone / inter-division transactions within Indian Railways

If inward supply is exempt, no GST is charged by supplier → no ITC question arises.

→ EX (No GST on inward — no ITC)→ NOT exempt: proceed to Step 4
4

Is it used EXCLUSIVELY for taxable supply (AC coaches / freight operations)?

100% identifiable use for AC coach maintenance, AC linen, OBHS-AC, on-board catering for AC, new AC rolling stock, P&M works contract for workshop, software AMC for taxable operations, new OHE/signalling for taxable freight/AC output.

→ T4 (Full ITC)→ NOT exclusively taxable: proceed to Step 5
5

Used for BOTH taxable and exempt supplies (common input)?

Telecom, security, station cleaning, GTA at 12%/18%, consultancy, bank charges, advertising, air travel, courier, running room, OBHS for mixed trains, P.Way service, OHE repair service — any input benefiting both AC (taxable) and Non-AC (exempt) operations.

Action: Flag C2. Reverse proportionately every month using Rule 42 formula. Annual true-up in September GSTR-3B. Excess credit @18% p.a. interest.

→ C2 (Partial ITC — Rule 42 monthly reversal)
