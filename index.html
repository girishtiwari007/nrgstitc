✅ T4 — P&M Exception to 17(5)

  • 9954 (P&M WC) — Works contract: signalling, workshop P&M, traction, OHE
  • 995466 — Lift installation at stations, FOBs
  • 995467 — Escalator installation
  • 9954 — Kavach / IBS / ASC / signalling system
  • 9954 — New OHE / traction electrification (P&M)
  • 9987 — AMC of Plant & Machinery (workshop)

Legal: Exception in Sec 17(5)(c) — "other than plant and machinery". Finance Act 2025 confirms P&M = "plant and machinery" (conjunctive) from 01.07.2017.

  • 995423 — HT lines: T3 if permanent; OFC ducts/manholes: C2 per CBIC 219/2024
  • 9954 P.Way — service portion C2; goods T3
  • 9954 OHE repair — service C2; goods T3
  • 9954 S&T repair — service C2; goods T3

Always separate goods and service components in split bills. Apply T3 for goods going into immovable; C2 for service used in mixed output.

🏗️ Capital Fund Works (Plan Heads 1100–6300)

  • New line / doubling / gauge conversion (civil) → T3
  • New station buildings / ROB / FOB → T3
  • New signalling / Kavach / IBS → T4 (P&M)
  • New OHE / traction electrification → T4 (P&M)
  • Workshop P&M (lathe, CNC, crane) → T4
  • Lifts & escalators at stations → T4
  • New rolling stock (AC/freight) → T4
  • New rolling stock (mixed fleet) → C2
  • Staff quarters / colonies → T3
  • DRF immovable replacement → T3
  • DRF P&M / rolling stock replacement → T4

🔧 Revenue Working Expenses (D3–D13)

  • P.Way maintenance (service) → C2
  • P.Way maintenance (goods/materials) → T3
  • OHE / S&T repair (service) → C2
  • OHE / S&T repair (goods) → T3
  • Workshop repair services → C2
  • Workshop repair goods (stock items) → T3
  • AC coach maintenance (dedicated) → T4
  • Station cleaning / security / OBHS → C2
  • Building maintenance (any) → T3
  • Non-AC exclusive inputs → T2
  • Staff welfare / medical / school → T1

💰 Deposit Works (Third-Party Funded)

  • ROB / RUB deposit (State Govt) → T3
    Asset = immovable bridge. T3 at IR IPAS.
  • Electrification deposit (OHE/traction) → T4
    OHE = P&M. T4 eligible.
  • Signalling deposit (Kavach/IBS) → T4
  • RVNL / DFCCIL / IRCON → EX
    CBIC 228/2024: exempt from GST w.e.f. 15.07.2024, regularised from 01.07.2017.
  • MoD deposit — immovable → T3
  • MoD deposit — P&M / equipment → T4

⚖️ Capitalised vs Revenue Repair

  • Capitalised repair (increases asset life, capitalised) → T3
    Finance Act 2025 Sec 124: "construction includes renovation to extent capitalised"
  • Revenue building repair (not capitalised) → T3
    WC on immovable = T3 always under Sec 17(5)(c)
  • Revenue P&M repair service → C2
  • Revenue P&M repair goods → T3 (RBA 19/2024)
  • New P&M (capital, exclusively taxable) → T4

Is inward supply for a NON-BUSINESS purpose?

Schools, hospitals, colony welfare, sports, staff recreation — no nexus with railway transport operations or any taxable output.

→ T1 (No ITC)→ NO: Step 2
2

Is it EXCLUSIVELY for exempt passenger service (Non-AC / Sleeper / 2nd class)?

Must be clearly identifiable as exclusively for exempt supply. Any doubt → go to Step 5.

→ T2 (No ITC)→ NO: Step 3
3

Is it listed in Section 17(5) blocked categories?

  • Motor vehicles / rent-a-cab / staff car hire [Sec 17(5)(a)/(b)] — GST now 18% w.e.f. 22.09.2025 but still T3
  • Works contract for IMMOVABLE property [Sec 17(5)(c)] — except P&M. Rate now 18% w.e.f. 22.09.2025 but T3 unchanged
  • Goods/services for immovable property on own account [Sec 17(5)(d)] — except P&M [Finance Act 2025]
  • Food & beverages / outdoor catering / club membership [Sec 17(5)(b)]
  • Health / life insurance of employees [Sec 17(5)(b)(iii)] — Note: individual health & life insurance NOW EXEMPT under GST 2.0. But employee group policies — verify exemption applicability
  • Leave Travel Concession (LTC) [Sec 17(5)(b)]
  • Goods for repair of rolling stock in workshop [RBA 19/2024 Sl.3]
  • Goods for P&M construction (tracks, sleepers, signalling) [RBA 19/2024 Sl.7]
  • Building AMC (to extent capitalised) [Finance Act 2025 Sec 124]
  • GTA at 5% GST [RBA 19/2024 Sl.9]
→ T3 (No ITC — Blocked)→ NOT blocked: Step 3A
3A

Is inward supply EXEMPT (CBIC 228/2024 or Notif 04/2024-CT(R))?

  • SPV services (RVNL, DFCCIL) to IR during concession period
  • Inter-zone / inter-division transactions within Indian Railways
→ EX (No GST charged — no ITC)→ NOT exempt: Step 4
4

Used EXCLUSIVELY for taxable supply (AC / freight operations)?

100% identifiable: AC coach maintenance, AC linen, OBHS-AC, catering AC trains, new AC rolling stock, P&M works contract for workshop, software AMC for taxable ops, new OHE/signalling for taxable output.

→ T4 (Full ITC)→ NOT exclusively taxable: Step 5
5

Used for BOTH taxable and exempt (common input)?

Telecom, security, station cleaning, GTA at 12%/18%, consultancy, bank charges, advertising, air travel, courier, running room, mixed train OBHS, P.Way service, OHE repair service — any input benefiting both AC (taxable) and Non-AC (exempt).

Action: Flag C2. Monthly Rule 42 reversal. Annual true-up in September GSTR-3B. Excess credit @18% p.a. interest (CGST Sec 50).

→ C2 (Partial ITC — Rule 42 monthly reversal)

🆕 RBA 27/2025 (GST Circular 06/2025) dated 19.09.2025 — GST 2.0 Rate Rationalisation
56th GST Council (03.09.2025) rationalized 4-tier structure → 2-tier: Standard Rate 18%, Merit Rate 5%, De-merit 40%. Effective: 22.09.2025 per Notifications 09–15/2025-CT(R) dated 17.09.2025.
